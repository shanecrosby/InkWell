Celebrating the written word

At InkWell Stationers, we are passionate about the art of writing and curate elegant stationery for you.

Inkwell Stationers, nestled in the heart of the city, is more than just a stationery shop; it's a sanctuary for those who cherish the timeless art of writing. Our boutique is a tribute to the enduring power of the written word, and we're dedicated to those who still believe in the magic of pen on paper. 

Every item in our collection is meticulously chosen to enrich the writing experience. From the moment you step into Inkwell Stationers, you're transported into a world where every journal, pen, and sheet of paper tells a story.

Our range includes elegant fountain pens, handcrafted journals, and bespoke paper goods, each reflecting a commitment to quality and craftsmanship. Whether you're a seasoned writer, a journal enthusiast, or someone who appreciates the finer things in life, our store is a haven where your passion for writing is not just understood, but celebrated.

At Inkwell Stationers, we believe that the act of writing is deeply personal and profoundly artistic. That's why we curate our products with an eye for beauty and functionality.
Our pens are more than writing instruments; they're heirlooms, designed to inspire and endure. The journals we offer are crafted to become intimate companions on your writing journey, their pages waiting to be filled with thoughts, dreams, and stories.
We also understand the importance of sustainability, ensuring that our paper goods are not only beautiful but environmentally conscious.

Our knowledgeable staff are fellow writing enthusiasts, always on hand to guide you in finding that perfect piece that resonates with your unique style. Inkwell Stationers isn't just a store; it's a community for those who hold the art of writing close to their hearts. Here, every purchase is an investment in keeping the tradition of handwritten expression alive and vibrant.

How long is shipping?

Our shipping typically takes 3-5 business days for domestic orders. For international orders, please allow 7-14 business days, depending on country/region. We strive to process and dispatch your order as quickly as possible, ensuring that your stationery items reach you in a timely manner.

What is your warranty?

At Inkwell Stationers, all our products come with a minimum 12-month warranty covering any manufacturing defects. This warranty includes issues with materials or workmanship but does not cover damages caused by misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear. If you encounter any issues, please contact us for support and guidance on warranty claims.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase, provided the items are unused, in their original condition, and with all packaging intact. To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team who will guide you through the process. Refunds will be processed upon receipt and inspection of the returned items.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement without any charge. If your order has already been processed or shipped, cancellation may not be possible. In such cases, you may return the order once received, following our return policy guidelines.

How do I contact you?

For any inquiries or support, please contact us via our online contact form on the website. Our customer service team is dedicated to assisting you and will respond to your query as soon as possible.

What are your shop opening hours?

Our shop open during standard retail hours: Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM, late to 9:00 PM on Thursdays, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. We welcome you to visit us during these hours to explore our exquisite collection of stationery.
